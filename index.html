ABOUT SEQTWO

SEQTWO is an anonymous messaging system. Each user gets a numeric ID.

Anyone who knows your ID can send you a message. You never have to reveal who you are.

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Your ID is generated and saved in your browser.
2. Share your ID with anyone.
3. They type your ID in Compose and send a message.
4. You see it in your Inbox next time you load the page.
PRIVACY: Messages are stored in shared storage. No account. No login. No tracking. IDs are random numbers.
NOTE: This app uses shared storage visible to all users of this artifact. Do not send sensitive information.
